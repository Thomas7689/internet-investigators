Internet

World Wide Web

Purpose

Is a global network that allows millions of devices to communicate with each other

Was made on the back of the internet that focuses on presenting information rather than directly connecting devices

Fuction

Provides a wide variety of online services from email to online gaming. Everything that happens online goes through the internet

Focuses on providing a way to access information specifically in text, images, and videos

Access to each

Allows devices to communicate. This can be done without the use of a browser

Accessing the web requires a browser such as Firefox or google chrome

Main Difference

The internet is the infrastructure in which devices can communicate

The world wide web is a collection of content that are displayed through browsers which are built on top of the internet
